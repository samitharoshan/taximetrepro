How TaxiMeter Pro Works

TaxiMeter Pro is a free, browser-based GPS taxi fare calculator that uses your device's built-in location services to track your journey in real time. There is no app to download and no account required — simply open the page, set your fare rates, and press Start Ride.

Advertisement
📍

Live GPS Tracking

Your browser's Geolocation API provides real-time position updates. TaxiMeter Pro uses the Haversine formula to calculate the exact distance traveled between each GPS fix, filtering out noise from GPS jitter under 5 meters.

🗺️

Interactive Map

The map is powered by OpenStreetMap and the Leaflet.js library. Your route is drawn on the map as a live polyline so you can see exactly where you have traveled throughout the ride.

💰

Customizable Fare Formula

Set your own base fare, per-kilometer rate, and per-minute rate. The fare is recalculated every second and displayed in large, easy-to-read digits both on the panel and on the live map badge.

🧾

Instant Ride Receipt

When you stop the ride, a full breakdown receipt is generated showing the base fare, distance charge, time charge, total distance traveled, and total fare — all in one place.

🔒

Privacy First

All GPS data is processed locally in your browser. Your location is never sent to any server, stored in any database, or shared with any third party. The map tiles are fetched directly from OpenStreetMap's servers.

📱

Works on Any Device

TaxiMeter Pro is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Any modern browser with GPS support — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge — is supported out of the box.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Step 1 – Set your fare rates: Enter the base fare, price per kilometer, and price per minute in the Fare Settings panel on the left. These values match your local taxi tariff.
  • Step 2 – Allow location access: When prompted by your browser, click "Allow" to grant the page access to your GPS signal.
  • Step 3 – Press Start Ride: The meter begins immediately. Distance and time accumulate, and the fare updates every second.
  • Step 4 – Press Stop: When the trip ends, press Stop. A full receipt is displayed with a line-by-line breakdown of your fare.
  • Step 5 – Reset for the next ride: Press the Reset (↺) button to clear all data and prepare for a new passenger.

The Fare Calculation Formula

The fare is calculated using a standard taxi metering formula:
Total Fare = Base Fare + (Distance in km × Rate per km) + (Duration in minutes × Rate per minute)

This formula mirrors the metering logic used by most licensed taxi companies worldwide, making TaxiMeter Pro suitable for drivers, passengers, and fleet operators who need a transparent, verifiable fare reference.

About TaxiMeter Pro

TaxiMeter Pro was built to give passengers, independent drivers, and small taxi operators a free, reliable, and transparent fare calculation tool that works without any subscription, registration, or app installation.

Taxi and rideshare passengers often wonder whether the fare on the meter matches the actual distance traveled. TaxiMeter Pro solves this problem by giving passengers an independent, GPS-verified fare reading on their own device, calculated in real time using the same mathematical model used by professional taxi meters.

Taxi drivers and operators can use TaxiMeter Pro as a backup meter, a training tool for new drivers, or a simple way to provide a digital receipt to passengers. The customizable fare settings allow drivers to input their local regulatory tariff so the numbers exactly match the official meter.

Technology Used

  • OpenStreetMap – Free, open-source map data contributed by volunteers worldwide.
  • Leaflet.js – A lightweight open-source JavaScript library for interactive maps.
  • HTML5 Geolocation API – Built into all modern browsers; no plugin required.
  • Haversine Formula – A trigonometric formula used to calculate the great-circle distance between two GPS coordinates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TaxiMeter Pro free?

Yes. TaxiMeter Pro is completely free to use. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no premium tiers.

Does TaxiMeter Pro store my location?

No. GPS data is processed entirely inside your browser and is never transmitted to any server. When you close the tab, all data is discarded.

How accurate is the GPS distance measurement?

Accuracy depends on your device's GPS hardware and signal quality. TaxiMeter Pro filters out GPS position jumps smaller than 5 meters to reduce the effect of GPS noise. In open-sky conditions with a modern smartphone, accuracy is typically within 5–10 meters.

Can I use it on an iPhone?

Yes. TaxiMeter Pro works on Safari on iOS. Make sure location access is enabled for your browser in your iPhone's Settings app under Privacy & Security → Location Services.

What currency does TaxiMeter Pro use?

The fare is displayed in whatever currency you enter in the settings. The dollar sign ($) is used as a generic symbol — you can treat it as representing your local currency (rupee, euro, pound, etc.).
