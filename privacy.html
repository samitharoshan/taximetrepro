Privacy Policy

We built TaxiMeter Pro with privacy at its core. Here's exactly what we collect — and what we don't.

Last updated: June 2025

🛡️ Short version: TaxiMeter Pro never stores, transmits, or sells your GPS location data. All location processing happens inside your browser. We do not require an account, email, or any personal information to use this tool.

1. Who We Are

TaxiMeter Pro ("we", "our", "us") operates the website at taximeter.online. We provide a free, browser-based GPS taxi fare calculator. Our contact email is hello@taximeter.online.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The following data is never collected, stored, or transmitted by TaxiMeter Pro:

  • Your GPS coordinates or location history
  • Your ride routes or trip distances
  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Your device's IMEI, phone number, or hardware identifiers
  • Payment information of any kind

3. How GPS Data Is Used

When you click "Start Ride," your browser requests access to your device's GPS through the standard W3C Geolocation API. This requires your explicit permission.

All GPS data is processed locally in your browser only. Your coordinates are used to: (a) display your position on the map, (b) calculate the distance travelled, and (c) compute your estimated fare. This data is never sent to any server.

When you close or refresh the page, all GPS data is immediately discarded.

4. Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site in aggregate. This may collect anonymized data such as:

  • Pages visited and session duration
  • Device type, operating system, and browser
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Referral source (how you found our site)

This data is anonymous and does not include your GPS location. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Advertising

TaxiMeter Pro displays advertisements through Google AdSense to fund the free operation of this service. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. This is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

You can manage your ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

6. Cookies

TaxiMeter Pro itself does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set their own cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • OpenStreetMap — Map tile data (OSM Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics — Anonymized usage analytics
  • Google AdSense — Advertising

8. Children's Privacy

TaxiMeter Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The tool requires no account creation and collects no personal data from any user.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@taximeter.online or visit our Contact page.
