Frequently Asked Questions

Is TaxiMeter Pro completely free?

Yes. TaxiMeter Pro is 100% free to use. There are no subscriptions, hidden fees, or premium tiers. The tool is supported by non-intrusive display advertising.

Does the app store my GPS location data?

No. Your GPS data is processed entirely within your browser. We never transmit, store, or sell your location data to any server or third party. Your ride remains completely private.

Can I use TaxiMeter Pro offline?

The fare calculation and GPS tracking works as long as your device has GPS access. However, the map tiles (OpenStreetMap) do require an internet connection to display.

How do I report a bug or inaccuracy?

Use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Please describe the issue, your device type, and browser version so we can reproduce and fix it quickly.

Can I request support for my local currency?

Absolutely. TaxiMeter Pro currently defaults to USD but you can enter any fare rates manually. We're working on a built-in currency selector — submit a feature request and we'll prioritize it.
